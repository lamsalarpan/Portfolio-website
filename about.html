About


I'm Arpan Lamsal, a passionate web developer and programmer from Nepal, currently pursuing my studies in grade 12 at Shishu Niketan Secondary English School in Pokhara, Kaski.

Since embarking on my programming journey in 2021, I've honed my skills in HTML, CSS, QBASIC, and C programming.

My dedication to creating responsive and user-friendly websites has led me to engage in part-time web development alongside my academic pursuits.

I'm always eager to embrace new challenges and continuously expand my skill set.

In addition to web development, I have experience in photo editing and graphic design, which complements my technical expertise.

I'm actively exploring freelancing opportunities to apply my knowledge to real-world projects and grow as a developer.

My educational journey has taken me through Friendship Public School in Magyam Chisapani, Syangja, and Panchamunidev Secondary School in Dulegaunda, Tanahun, before my current enrollment at Shishu Niketan Secondary English School.

I'm committed to leveraging my skills to contribute to innovative projects and am open to collaboration.

Feel free to explore my work, and if you have any projects in mind or wish to collaborate, don't hesitate to reach out!
